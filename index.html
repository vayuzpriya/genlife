Most Life Insurance Doesn't Help You When You Need It Most

Traditional life insurance only pays after you die. A Living Benefits policy pays you — up to 80% of your death benefit — if you're diagnosed with a serious illness while still living.

Imagine having a heart attack next month.
Would your current policy help you pay a single bill?

Traditional life insurance pays your family after you die — it pays you nothing while you're fighting to survive. A Living Benefits policy gives you early access to up to 80% of your death benefit to cover bills, mortgage, and lost income — no repayment required.

✓ Your family still receives the remaining death benefit at your passing.
✓ No loans — it's your own money from your own policy.
✓ No extra premium in most cases.

Traditional Life Insurance

Pays a lump sum to your family only after you die. If you have a heart attack, cancer, or stroke — your policy pays you absolutely nothing while you're still alive and fighting.

Living Benefits Insurance

Access up to 80% of your death benefit while still alive if diagnosed with cancer, a heart attack, stroke, or chronic/terminal illness. Same or comparable premium — no extra cost in most policies.

1 in 2 Americans will face a serious illness in their lifetime. Traditional life insurance pays nothing during treatment. Living Benefits exist to fix this gap.
